About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Trattoria Verde began as a family restaurant in Florence. We grew while keeping our traditional recipes alive.

Our Philosophy

We believe in simplicity — fresh ingredients, handcrafted dishes, and a warm atmosphere. Everything is prepared from scratch with local ingredients when possible.

Our Team

Our chefs and staff combine decades of Mediterranean, Sicilian, and Tuscan cuisine experience to deliver hospitality and flavor.
